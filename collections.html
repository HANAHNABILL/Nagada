Threads of the
Collections

A cinematic exploration of heritage textiles. Each line is a curated dialogue between ancient loom techniques and contemporary sculptural silhouettes.

Line 01
FAFI

The Farka Line: Traditional Meets Avant-Garde

FAFI reinterprets the rustic density of West African Farka weaving into modern, architectural silhouettes. It is a study in weight, gravity, and the intentional fray.

Line 02
RIRI

The Double Face Line

Transformation is not an event, but a perspective.
Turn the fabric, change the narrative.

A visual play on duality. Each RIRI piece features two distinct faces—one a raw, matte weave, the other a luminous silk finish.

Explore Archive
Line 03
GIGI

The Fobram Line

Structural Compositions

GiGi explores the intersection of origami and textile. Fabrics are treated to hold memory, creating wearable sculptures that defy the traditional draping of cloth.
