The Hands
That Weave

In the quiet village of Naqada, the rhythm of the loom has been the heartbeat of the community for centuries. We follow the story of Amina, a master weaver whose family has kept the secret of the "Silver Warp" for five generations. Each movement is a dance of precision and patience.

The Vocabulary of Silk

The Meaning of
Farka

The Farka is more than a garment. It is a ceremonial passage. Historically worn by brides and royalty, its complexity lies in the shifting colors of the silk threads that change depending on the angle of the light.

We explore the mathematical precision required to create the diamond pattern, a motif that signifies protection and eternal flow. Each piece takes over 120 hours of concentrated labor on the loom.
